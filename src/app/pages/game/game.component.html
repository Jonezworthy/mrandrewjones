Pokémon or Tech?

This is a game, inspired by a reddit post where someone would get recruiters to say whether they thought some of the tech on their CV was Pokémon

How to play

You are shown a name one at a time, and you have to guess whether it is something from the world of tech, or whether it is a Pokémon character.

Before starting you choose how many names you wish to play with.
